Healthcare and Medical Diagnostics Innovation Driven by AI

Social Status

As AI large models gradually move towards the application level, various fields have sparked a wave of AI applications. Currently, the industry has a clear definition of the combination of AI and medical care. For example, from the perspective of medical apparatus, according to the definition of the pharmaceutical industry standard "Quality Requirements and Evaluation of Artificial Intelligence Medical Apparatus: Terminology", artificial intelligence medical apparatus refers to medical apparatus that uses artificial intelligence technology implementation for its intended use, such as medical apparatus software that uses technology implementation such as Machine Learning, neural networks, and Deep Learning to assist in diagnosis, as well as medical apparatus software that empowers medical apparatus from the source with artificial intelligence technology to form intelligent hardware devices embedded with AI algorithms or based on AI chips. In summary, the combination of AI and medical care is the process of fully empowering medical scenarios using advanced algorithms and carriers of AI, continuously achieving smarter and more accurate diagnosis, treatment, and management.

With the steady growth of the national economy and the rapid progress of life sciences, the average life expectancy of Chinese residents has been significantly improved. In 2019, this number has reached 77.3 years, an increase of 33 years compared to 1960. Driven by multiple factors such as population aging, people's demand for health continues to rise. It is expected that by 2050, the elderly population aged 60 and above in China will account for 34.9% of the total population, which will pose higher challenges to the health system, including efficiency requirements in diagnosis, treatment, and management. Against this backdrop, the informatization, digitization, and intelligence of the medical industry have become an inevitable trend of development.

As of December 1, 2023, 122 intelligent software have obtained Market Access in China. The total number of review and approval reports released by the National Medical Products Administration in the first 11 months of 2023 is 48, surpassing the 32 in 2022.

According to statistics, from August 31, 2022 to October 31, 2023, the medical AI sector completed a total of 170 financings, with a total financing amount of 16.124 billion yuan. Medical artificial intelligence has entered a steady development period.

Investigate three representative medical AI companies at home and abroad, namely Yingtong Technology, Lunit, and Nanox AI. In terms of revenue, Yingtong Technology remained the same as last year, while Lunit and Nano-X Imaging achieved a certain scale of growth.

Imaging AI Market - The AI-assisted medical imaging market is growing rapidly

Artificial intelligence AI technology covers multiple fields such as Computer Vision, Knowledge Graph, and natural language processing. Its main application areas include medical image analysis, clinical decision support systems (CDSS), new drug development, and intelligent medical record management. Especially in the field of medical imaging, such as radiology, pathology, ultrasound, etc., artificial intelligence AI technology is playing an important role in promoting the accuracy, consistency, and efficiency of image diagnosis, and promoting the development of medical imaging towards more accurate, standardized, and efficient direction.

China's artificial intelligence AI medical market will continue to grow rapidly in the next decade, and artificial intelligence AI medical imaging (including X-ray, CT, pathology, ultrasound, etc.) will become the fastest growing segment market.

In the field of radiological imaging analysis, artificial intelligence AI technology has been widely used in the screening of diseases such as rib fractures, pulmonary nodules, and pneumonia, with the aim of reducing missed diagnoses and improving the speed and accuracy of diagnosis. At the same time, artificial intelligence AI technology is also used to evaluate diseases such as intracranial hemorrhage, functional myocardial ischemia, coronary artery stenosis, and head and neck artery stenosis, providing decision support for clinical doctors to optimize treatment plans and improve patient treatment outcomes.

Since 2015, the application of artificial intelligence AI in the field of radiographic imaging has developed rapidly. As of July 2022, 6 companies have obtained 7 Class III registration certificates for blood flow-related artificial intelligence AI software; 10 companies have obtained 14 Class III registration certificates for artificial intelligence AI software for lung CT applications; and another 3 companies have obtained 3 Class III registration certificates for bone X-ray applications. This indicates that the performance of these artificial intelligence AI software, including its sensitivity and specificity, has passed the validation of clinical trials and the approval of the Drug Administration, indicating that these products have entered the monetization stage. China's artificial intelligence AI radiographic imaging market is growing rapidly at a compound annual growth rate of 50 to 60%, becoming an important auxiliary diagnostic tool in clinical settings.

On the one hand, AI has made significant progress in medical imaging diagnosis. Through Deep learning and Computer Vision technology, AI can quickly and accurately analyze and diagnose a large amount of medical imaging data, helping doctors improve diagnostic efficiency and accuracy. This greatly shortens the time for patients to wait for diagnosis results, helps to detect diseases early and provide faster treatment plans.

On the other hand, AI also shows great potential in personalized treatment. By analyzing the patient's genomic data and case history, AI can develop personalized treatment plans for patients, including drug selection, dose adjustment, etc., thereby improving treatment effectiveness and reducing the risk of drug side effects.

On March 29th, 2024, researchers from Arizona State University published a research paper titled The electrostatic landscape of MHC-peptide binding revealed using inception networks in the Cell journal Cell Systems.

The study developed a tool based on artificial intelligence (AI) called HLA Inception. The AI tool focuses on a group of proteins called Major Histocompatibility Complex-1 (MHC-1), which can classify specific protein groups in seconds and predict whether a person's immune system can recognize protein fragments from viruses or cancer. Understanding this personalized molecular interaction information has great potential to create new personalized cancer drugs, which may change patient treatment.

In order to promote scientific progress in this field, the research team has provided HLA-Inception for free to the academic community, laying the foundation for extensive collaboration and innovation in the field of immunotherapy. The research team envisions that this work will help advance healthcare, especially in tailoring treatment methods for patients.

Future and Outlook

In addition to the field of clinical medicine, AI has also injected new vitality into traditional Chinese medicine.

Currently, public healthcare in Hong Kong mainly consists of 43 public hospitals, 49 specialist clinics, 73 general clinics, and nearly 100 Chinese medicine clinics, public dental clinics, and infirmaries of all sizes. The capacity and capacity of public healthcare in Hong Kong are limited. According to a set of data released by Frost & Sullivan, from July 2019 to July 2020, the waiting period for most public specialist outpatient appointments has exceeded 50 weeks. According to statistics from the Hospital Authority in 2020, the number of Chinese medicine clinics and research centers (TCM clinics) in the 18 districts of Hong Kong has been increasing. Last year, more than 1.50 million people visited TCM clinics in the 18 districts, an increase of nearly 30% compared to 2018. In addition, the aging population trend has also led to an increase in demand for TCM.

The Four Diagnostic Instruments of Traditional Chinese Medicine have emerged. It is an intelligent diagnostic instrument developed based on traditional Chinese medicine theory and modern technology. It comprehensively understands the patient's condition through four diagnostic methods: observation, listening, questioning, and cutting, providing doctors with more accurate and objective diagnostic basis. The Four Diagnostic Instruments of Traditional Chinese Medicine have the characteristics of easy operation, high accuracy, and wide range of use, which can provide strong support for medical workers.

In the future, with the continuous advancement of technology and the active attempts of the medical industry, the application prospects of AI in the medical field are bright. Through more research and exploration, AI is expected to bring profound changes to various aspects such as medical diagnosis, treatment, and drug development, bringing greater benefits and changes to human health and medical care.
